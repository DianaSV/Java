David Batt

                David Michael Blatt (Hebrew: דוד מיכאל בלאט‎; born May 22, 1959),
                 is an Israeli American professional basketball executive.
                He is also a former coach and player.
                Blatt played point guard at Princeton University from 1977 to 1981
                and played in the Maccabiah Games for the U.S. national team that won a gold medal in 1981.
